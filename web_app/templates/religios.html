Our Religious Ethos

At Crescent Heights, we embrace the spiritual growth of our students alongside their academic achievements. Our religious programs are designed to foster a deep sense of spirituality, morality, and community service.

Daily Prayers

We hold daily prayer sessions for students to reflect and seek guidance. These sessions help in instilling values of humility and gratitude.

MSSN Weekly Program

Our curriculum includes religious education classes that cover a wide range of topics from various religious traditions, promoting understanding and respect for diverse beliefs.

Community Service

Engagement in community service projects is a core part of our religious programs, encouraging students to live out their faith through acts of kindness and service to others.

Juma'ah Service

We host interfaith dialogues to promote mutual respect and understanding among students of different religious backgrounds, fostering a peaceful and inclusive school environment.
