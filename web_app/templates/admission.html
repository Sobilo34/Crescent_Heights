Admission Process

Congratulations on beginning your journey with Crescent Heights Oyo. We understand the importance of choosing the right school for your children. The process outlined below is typical but can be adapted to ensure that you and your child are supported every step of the way.

Step 1:

The registrar will discuss your initial inquiry and provide you with an application form. For other years, please contact the admissions office to discuss a suitable date.

Step 2:

Complete the application form and submit it to the admission office.

Step 3:

For entry, make initial contact with the school's admission office.

Step 4:

Click Here to download the Application form.

Age Consideration

Crescent Heights recommends that students enter Class One after their tenth birthday. Entering before this age may lead to early university admission before the age of sixteen. We believe the best time to enter secondary school is during adolescence, especially for hostel students.

It provides a conducive environment for personal growth, social development, and academic success. Our holistic approach to education ensures that students are prepared for the challenges of the future.
