Co-Curricullum Activities

Home Co-Curricullum Activities

Crescent Heights offers ample opportunities for students to engage in various clubs:

PRESS CLUB

The Press Club aims to:

  1. To facilitate discussions on current affairs.
  2. To provide a platform for journalistic endeavors.
  3. To foster critical thinking and communication skills.
  4. To promote media literacy among students.



YOUNG FARMERS CLUB

The Young Farmers Club objectives include:

  1. To promote agricultural awareness and practices.
  2. To engage students in sustainable farming initiatives.
  3. To provide hands-on experience in farming and gardening.
  4. To educate about environmental conservation in agriculture.



JUNIOR ENGINEERS, TECHNICIANS AND SCIENTISTS (JETs) CLUB

The JETs Club aims to:

  1. To foster interest and skills in engineering, technology, and science.
  2. To provide hands-on experience through projects and experiments.
  3. To encourage innovation and problem-solving abilities among students.
  4. To prepare students for future careers in STEM fields.



CATERING CLUB

The Catering Club objectives include:

  1. To develop culinary skills and knowledge among students.
  2. To promote healthy eating habits and nutrition awareness.
  3. To provide opportunities for practical experience in food preparation and service.
  4. To explore diverse cuisines and culinary traditions.
